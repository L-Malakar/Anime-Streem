Our Story

BUILT BY FANS.
FOR FANS.

ANIvalted was born out of frustration. Too many sites. Too many ads. Too many broken links and suspicious popups. We wanted one place — clean, fast, honest — where any anime fan in India and beyond could find their favourite series, discover hidden gems, and watch without shame or hassle. So we built it.

We believe anime is more than entertainment — it's culture, philosophy, emotion. Demon Slayer isn't just a fight scene; it's a meditation on grief. Frieren isn't just a fantasy; it's a question about what makes a life meaningful. Stories like these deserve to be accessible to everyone — in every language, at no cost.
